============
Ingredients
============

1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 1/2  teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
8 to 9 cups thinly sliced peeled tart apples
1 tablespoon lemon juice
Pastry for double-crust pie (9 inches)
1 tablespoon butter
1 large egg white
27g sugar

============
Directions
============

In a small bowl, combine the sugars, flour and spices; set aside. 
In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat.
Line a 9-in. pie plate with bottom crust; trim crust even with edge. 
Fill with apple mixture; dot with butter. 
Roll out remaining dough to fit top of pie. Place over filling. 
Trim, seal and flute edges. Cut slits in crust.
Beat egg white until foamy; brush over crust. 
Sprinkle with sugar. Cover edges loosely with foil.
Bake at 375° for 25 minutes. 
Remove foil and bake until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly, 20-25 minutes longer. 
Cool on a wire rack.
